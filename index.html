Hello! I'm a second year PhD student at the University of Waterloo's Cheriton School of Computer Science, where I'm very fortunate to be a member of The Salon and be advised by Gautam Kamath. My interests broadly lie at the intersection of Theoretical Computer Science and Statistics, with a particular focus on Algorithmic Statistics, Machine Learning Theory and Differential Privacy. Before joining UWaterloo, I did my undergraduate studies in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens in Greece, where I was a member of CoReLab and was advised by Dimitris Fotakis.
